# Usage Guide

## Analytics Dashboard

### Accessing Analytics

1. From your **Dashboard**, find the landing page you want to analyze
2. Click the **Analytics** button (ðŸ“Š icon) on the page card
3. The Analytics Dashboard will open showing real-time metrics

### Understanding Your Metrics

**Page Views**: Total number of times your page was loaded
**Unique Visitors**: Number of distinct individuals who visited
**Conversions**: Total form submissions or CTA clicks  
**Conversion Rate**: Percentage of visitors who converted

### Viewing Traffic Sources

1. Click the **Traffic Sources** tab
2. See breakdown of where your visitors come from:
   - Direct traffic
   - Organic search
   - Social media
   - Referral links
3. Each source shows conversion rate to help you optimize your marketing spend

### Date Range Selection

Switch between different time periods using the buttons:
- **7 Days**: Last week's performance
- **30 Days**: Monthly trends
- **90 Days**: Quarterly overview

### Tips for Success

- Publish your page to start collecting analytics data
- Add UTM parameters to your marketing links to track campaign performance
- Check analytics regularly to spot trends and opportunities
- Compare conversion rates across traffic sources to focus your efforts

## Template Library

### Browsing Templates

1. Click **Templates** in the dashboard header
2. Browse through 50+ professionally designed templates
3. Use the search bar to find specific styles or industries
4. Filter by category: SaaS, E-commerce, Real Estate, Events, etc.

### Using a Template

1. Click on any template card to see more details
2. Review the template description and tags
3. Click **Use Template** to import it
4. The template will be copied to your account as a new landing page
5. You'll be redirected to the editor to customize it

### Template Categories

**SaaS**: Software and B2B product launches
**E-commerce**: Product showcases and online stores  
**Real Estate**: Property listings and agent profiles
**Events**: Conference and webinar registrations
**Education**: Online courses and coaching services
**Healthcare**: Medical services and wellness programs
**Finance**: Financial services and consultations

### Customizing Templates

After importing a template:
1. Edit text by clicking on any section
2. Upload your own images or generate them with AI
3. Adjust colors to match your brand
4. Add or remove sections as needed
5. Publish when ready

### Tips for Using Templates

- Start with a template that matches your industry
- Don't be afraid to mix and match sections from different templates
- Keep your brand colors and fonts consistent
- Test your page on mobile devices before publishing
- Look at highly-rated templates for inspiration

## Quick Start Workflow

### Creating Your First Landing Page from a Template

1. **Login** to your dashboard
2. Click **Templates** in the header
3. Browse or search for a template that fits your needs
4. Click **Use Template** on your chosen template
5. **Customize** the content in the editor
6. Click **Publish** when ready
7. **Share** your page link
8. **Monitor** performance in the Analytics Dashboard

### Optimizing Based on Analytics

1. Wait for traffic to accumulate (minimum 100 visitors recommended)
2. Check your **conversion rate** in Analytics
3. Identify **low-performing traffic sources** 
4. Review **engagement** metrics (bounce rate, session duration)
5. Make **targeted improvements** to your page
6. **Monitor changes** in analytics to measure impact
7. **Iterate** based on data

## FAQs

**Q: When will I see analytics data?**  
A: Analytics start collecting as soon as you publish your page and get your first visitor.

**Q: Can I use multiple templates?**  
A: Yes! Import as many templates as you want and customize each one.

**Q: How do I track specific campaigns?**  
A: Add UTM parameters to your links (e.g., `?utm_source=facebook&utm_campaign=summer_sale`)

**Q: Can I export my analytics data?**  
A: Yes, use the Export button in the Analytics dashboard to download CSV reports.

**Q: Are templates free?**  
A: Currently, all templates in the library are free to use. Premium templates may be added in the future.

**Q: Can I customize templates completely?**  
A: Absolutely! Templates are starting points. You have full editing control over every element.
